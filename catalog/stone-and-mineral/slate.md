# Slate

- **Visual Description:** blue-gray to charcoal stone with thin parallel sheets and sharp chipped edges. Its surface has directional cleavage lines.
- **Procedural Identity:** anisotropic layer bands, long thin cracks, flat plate height steps.
- **Useful Variations:** like basalt, but flatter, bluer, and strongly layered.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.5-0.85; directional normal.
