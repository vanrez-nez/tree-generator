# Granite

- **Visual Description:** hard speckled stone with interlocked black, white, gray, and pink mineral grains. Pattern is salt-and-pepper crystalline, not veined.
- **Procedural Identity:** multi-scale crystalline cells, sharp color flecks, low rounded relief, rare darker seams.
- **Useful Variations:** like concrete, but crystalline and less dusty; like marble, but speckled instead of veined.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.45-0.8; low normal unless rough-cut.
