# Obsidian

- **Visual Description:** black volcanic glass, #050505 to #1a1614, with sharp conchoidal fracture curves and glossy highlights baked into roughness variation.
- **Procedural Identity:** very dark base, curved fracture arcs, smoky brown edge tints, low roughness patches.
- **Useful Variations:** like basalt, but glassier, smoother, and broken by curved shell-like fractures.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.05-0.35; medium fracture normal.
