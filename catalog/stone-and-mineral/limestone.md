# Limestone

- **Visual Description:** pale beige, cream, or light gray stone with soft fossil-like flecks and cloudy patches. Surface feels chalky and sedimentary.
- **Procedural Identity:** low-contrast cloudy strata, tiny shell fragments, occasional horizontal bedding lines.
- **Useful Variations:** like marble, but duller, grainier, and without high-contrast veins.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.65-0.95; low to medium normal.
