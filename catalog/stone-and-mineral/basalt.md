# Basalt

- **Visual Description:** dark gray to black volcanic rock, #17191a to #45484a, often fine-grained with angular cracks and occasional tiny pores.
- **Procedural Identity:** dark matte base, fracture-line network, subtle vesicle holes, low color saturation.
- **Useful Variations:** like slate, but chunkier, darker, and less layered.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.55-0.9; medium normal; strong crack AO.
