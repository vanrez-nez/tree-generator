# Marble

- **Visual Description:** polished or honed white, gray, green, or black stone with branching translucent veins. Veins are cloudy, feathered, and irregular rather than straight cracks.
- **Procedural Identity:** warped vein network, soft subsurface-looking color blooms, low pore noise, optional polish mask.
- **Useful Variations:** like limestone, but smoother, denser, and crossed by stronger veins.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.15-0.55 depending polish; low normal; veins may have subtle height.
