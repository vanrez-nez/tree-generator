# Crystal Vein Stone

- **Visual Description:** ordinary gray or brown host rock crossed by exposed colored crystal seams, such as cyan, violet, amber, or emerald. The crystal veins are embedded, not separate objects.
- **Procedural Identity:** stone substrate plus masked vein channels, angular crystal facets inside veins, optional emissive tint for fantasy variants.
- **Useful Variations:** like marble, but veins are harder-edged, brighter, and mineral-filled rather than cloudy.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic host; roughness contrast between host and crystal; optional emission.
