# Travertine

- **Visual Description:** beige to honey limestone-like stone with elongated pores, bands, and small cavities. It looks porous and warm rather than crystalline.
- **Procedural Identity:** directional elongated holes, cream/gold banding, cavity AO, low rounded ridges.
- **Useful Variations:** like marble, but warmer, more porous, and less veined.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.45-0.85; medium pore normal.
