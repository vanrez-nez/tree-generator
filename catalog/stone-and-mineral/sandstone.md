# Sandstone

- **Visual Description:** tan, ochre, or muted red stone with visible horizontal sediment layers and gritty pores. It looks like compacted sand.
- **Procedural Identity:** layered band noise, fine granular bump, small darker mineral streaks.
- **Useful Variations:** like sand, but cemented into stone and organized in visible strata.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.85-1.0; fine normal plus broad layer height.
