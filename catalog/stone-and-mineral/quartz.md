# Quartz

- **Visual Description:** milky white, rose, smoky gray, or clear-looking mineral with cloudy internal patches and angular crystalline breaks. As a baked material it should read waxy-glassy but not refractive.
- **Procedural Identity:** faceted angular cells, cloudy color gradients, sharp crack inclusions, low roughness facets.
- **Useful Variations:** like marble, but more crystalline, less veined, and with sharper translucent-looking planes.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.12-0.45; low to medium faceted normal.
