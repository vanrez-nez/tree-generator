# Painted Chipped Metal

- **Visual Description:** colored paint layer over metal with scratches, chips, and exposed silver or rusty underlayer. Paint may be matte red, blue, green, yellow, or white.
- **Procedural Identity:** base paint mask, edge-chip noise, scratch streaks, exposed metal/rust under chips.
- **Useful Variations:** like painted wood, but chips reveal reflective metal and rust rather than pale fibers.
- **Typical Channels:** paint metallic 0.0, exposed metal 1.0; roughness contrast; strong chip AO.
