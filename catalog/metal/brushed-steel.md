# Brushed Steel

- **Visual Description:** cool silver metal, #9aa0a3 to #d1d5d6, with dense parallel hairline scratches in one direction. Reflections appear stretched along the brush axis.
- **Procedural Identity:** anisotropic scratch bands, very fine directional normal, slight blue-gray color ramp.
- **Useful Variations:** like polished iron, but lighter, cleaner, and directionally scratched.
- **Typical Channels:** metallic 1.0; roughness 0.22-0.55; directional normal.
