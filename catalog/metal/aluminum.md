# Aluminum

- **Visual Description:** light cool-gray metal, #b8bec0 to #e0e4e4, often matte with soft white highlights and low color saturation.
- **Procedural Identity:** pale metal base, cloudy oxidation, fine scratches, optional brushed direction.
- **Useful Variations:** like steel, but lighter, less blue, and softer-looking.
- **Typical Channels:** metallic 1.0; roughness 0.25-0.75.
