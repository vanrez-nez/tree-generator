# Rusted Metal

- **Visual Description:** dark metal broken by orange, red, and brown corrosion, #6a2815 to #c05a20, with flaky raised patches and black pits.
- **Procedural Identity:** layered rust masks, high-point flakes, cavity pits, non-metallic oxide zones.
- **Useful Variations:** like cast iron, but colored by flaky orange corrosion.
- **Typical Channels:** mixed metallic: exposed metal 1.0, rust 0.0; roughness 0.75-1.0 on rust; strong normal.
