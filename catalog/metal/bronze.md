# Bronze

- **Visual Description:** muted brown-gold metal, #7a4f25 to #b9853c, less red than copper and darker than brass. Aged bronze shows green patina in cracks.
- **Procedural Identity:** warm metallic base, dark brown oxidation, sparse green cavity deposits.
- **Useful Variations:** like copper, but browner, deeper, and less orange.
- **Typical Channels:** metallic 1.0 on exposed bronze; roughness 0.25-0.7.
