# Copper

- **Visual Description:** warm orange-red metal, #b15a2b to #e08a48, with soft darker smudges and occasional pink highlights. Fresh copper is smooth and saturated.
- **Procedural Identity:** metallic warm ramp, subtle tarnish masks, fine handling scratches.
- **Useful Variations:** like bronze, but redder, brighter, and less brown.
- **Typical Channels:** metallic 1.0; roughness 0.18-0.55; low normal.
