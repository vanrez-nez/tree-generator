# Gunmetal

- **Visual Description:** dark blue-gray metal, #24282d to #555b61, satin rather than mirror polished. It has fine machining marks and subdued highlights.
- **Procedural Identity:** cool dark metallic ramp, fine random scratches, medium roughness uniformity.
- **Useful Variations:** like blackened steel, but bluer and smoother.
- **Typical Channels:** metallic 1.0; roughness 0.25-0.55.
