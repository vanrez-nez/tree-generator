# Gold

- **Visual Description:** saturated warm yellow metal, #d9a528 to #ffd36a, with very low oxidation and smooth reflective areas. Dirty gold darkens in scratches rather than rusting.
- **Procedural Identity:** stable yellow metallic color, low roughness, fine scratches, dark grime masks only in cavities.
- **Useful Variations:** like brass, but richer, cleaner, and less green.
- **Typical Channels:** metallic 1.0; roughness 0.08-0.35.
