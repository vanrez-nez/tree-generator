# Cast Iron

- **Visual Description:** dark rough metal, #1f2020 to #484845, with pitted pores, tiny bumps, and matte oxidized gray patches.
- **Procedural Identity:** high-frequency pits, dark cavity AO, uneven roughness, minimal linear scratching.
- **Useful Variations:** like blackened steel, but rougher, grainier, and more porous.
- **Typical Channels:** metallic 0.8-1.0; roughness 0.55-0.9; medium pitted normal.
