# Oxidized Copper

- **Visual Description:** copper base partially covered by blue-green patina, #2f8f82 to #83b99a, collecting in seams and spreading as powdery islands.
- **Procedural Identity:** patina mask in cavities and streaks, exposed orange metal on worn high points, rougher oxide than metal.
- **Useful Variations:** like copper, but aged with matte turquoise corrosion concentrated in recesses.
- **Typical Channels:** metal exposed areas 1.0, patina 0.0-0.2; roughness 0.45-0.95; medium oxide height.
