# Brass

- **Visual Description:** yellow-gold metal, #b58a34 to #e0bf62, with softer saturation than pure gold and frequent brown tarnish.
- **Procedural Identity:** yellow metallic ramp, smudged roughness, dark tarnish in creases.
- **Useful Variations:** like gold, but duller, greener-yellow, and more industrial.
- **Typical Channels:** metallic 1.0; roughness 0.18-0.65.
