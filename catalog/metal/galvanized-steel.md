# Galvanized Steel

- **Visual Description:** pale silver-gray metal with irregular crystalline zinc flakes, like overlapping frost patches. Pattern has angular cloudy cells, not scratches.
- **Procedural Identity:** large zinc spangle cells, alternating light/dark flake orientation, low relief.
- **Useful Variations:** like brushed steel, but mottled into broad crystal patches instead of linear grain.
- **Typical Channels:** metallic 1.0; roughness 0.35-0.65; very low normal.
