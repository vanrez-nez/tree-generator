# Polished Iron

- **Visual Description:** dark neutral gray metal, #2f3335 to #6f7475, with broad smooth tonal gradients and minimal scratches. It reads heavy and reflective.
- **Procedural Identity:** metallic base, low roughness, sparse fine scratches, subtle dark edge grime.
- **Useful Variations:** like polished steel, but darker, warmer, and less clean.
- **Typical Channels:** metallic 1.0; roughness 0.12-0.35; low normal.
