# Vinyl

- **Visual Description:** flexible synthetic sheet, often black, white, or colored, with smooth surface, slight orange-peel texture, and broad creases.
- **Procedural Identity:** low pore detail, crease lines, moderate gloss, uniform color.
- **Useful Variations:** like leather, but smoother, colder, and less organically cracked.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.25-0.65; low crease normal.
