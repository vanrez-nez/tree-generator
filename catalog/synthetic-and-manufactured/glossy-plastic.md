# Glossy Plastic

- **Visual Description:** saturated plastic with clearer highlights and smoother surface than matte plastic. Fingerprint smudges or fine scratches can break reflection.
- **Procedural Identity:** low roughness base, smudge masks, subtle scratch fields.
- **Useful Variations:** like acrylic, but softer and less glass-like.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.08-0.35; low normal.
