# Matte Plastic

- **Visual Description:** smooth non-metal surface in arbitrary saturated or neutral color, with soft highlights, subtle molding speckles, and no pores.
- **Procedural Identity:** flat color, fine micro-scratch noise, low height, optional injection flow marks.
- **Useful Variations:** like painted metal, but non-metallic, smoother, and without chipped reflective underlayer.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.45-0.8; very low normal.
