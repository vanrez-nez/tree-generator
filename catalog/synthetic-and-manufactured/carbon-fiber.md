# Carbon Fiber

- **Visual Description:** dark woven composite with alternating black and charcoal strands in a tight diagonal or checker weave. It reads technical and glossy.
- **Procedural Identity:** woven strand pattern, alternating strand brightness, clearcoat-like roughness mask baked statically.
- **Useful Variations:** like black fabric, but glossier, tighter, and more synthetic.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic or low metallic; roughness 0.18-0.5; fine weave normal.
