# Acrylic

- **Visual Description:** smooth colored or milky plastic sheet, represented without refraction. It has clean color, very low surface grain, and glossy roughness variation.
- **Procedural Identity:** flat clean base, subtle scuffs, low roughness, no pores.
- **Useful Variations:** like glossy plastic, but cleaner, harder, and more glass-like without transparency dependency.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.03-0.25; minimal normal.
