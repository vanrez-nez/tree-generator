# Fiberglass

- **Visual Description:** resin-coated material with faint woven glass fibers under a smooth or slightly bumpy surface, often off-white, yellowed, or colored.
- **Procedural Identity:** submerged weave pattern, cloudy resin stains, fine scratches.
- **Useful Variations:** like carbon fiber, but paler, duller, and resin-clouded.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.3-0.75; fine weave normal.
