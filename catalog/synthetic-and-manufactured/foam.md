# Foam

- **Visual Description:** soft porous synthetic material, usually gray, black, white, or colored, with rounded open pores or fine sponge texture.
- **Procedural Identity:** cellular pore noise, soft low relief, matte color.
- **Useful Variations:** like cork, but synthetic, softer, and often more uniform.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.9-1.0; medium pore normal.
