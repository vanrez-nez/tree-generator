# Sci-Fi Panel

- **Visual Description:** manufactured hard surface with panel seams, inset grooves, colored warning strips, screws, vents, or emissive lines baked as surface features.
- **Procedural Identity:** hard rectangular panel layout, bevel-like edge masks, scratches, dirt in seams, optional emission strips.
- **Useful Variations:** like painted chipped metal, but cleaner, modular, and more graphic.
- **Typical Channels:** mixed metallic/non-metallic by panel type; roughness 0.25-0.85; strong seam normal; optional emission.
