# Rubber

- **Visual Description:** black or dark colored matte elastic material, #050505 to #303030, with fine stippled texture or tread patterns.
- **Procedural Identity:** dark base, high roughness, stipple/pore bump, optional directional tread grooves.
- **Useful Variations:** like matte plastic, but darker, softer-looking, and grippier.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.75-1.0; low to medium normal.
