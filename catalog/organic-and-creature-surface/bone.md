# Bone

- **Visual Description:** ivory to yellowed off-white, #d8c79c to #f0e4c6, with pores, subtle longitudinal grain, and darker worn cracks.
- **Procedural Identity:** fine pore speckles, long organic streaks, dark crack lines, polished high areas.
- **Useful Variations:** like ivory, but more porous, less smooth, and more weathered.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.45-0.85; low to medium pore normal.
