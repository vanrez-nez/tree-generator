# Scales

- **Visual Description:** overlapping small plates in reptile-like rows, with darker gaps and color gradients across each scale. Colors can be green, brown, gray, red, or fantasy hues.
- **Procedural Identity:** repeated scale cells, overlap edge AO, per-scale color jitter, raised centers.
- **Useful Variations:** like tile, but smaller, organic, and overlapping.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.35-0.85; strong scale normal.
