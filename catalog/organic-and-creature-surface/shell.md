# Shell

- **Visual Description:** hard organic surface with cream, pink, brown, or blue-gray bands, fine ridges, and pearly patches baked as color variation.
- **Procedural Identity:** growth rings, radial ridges, pearlescent-looking color bands without view dependency.
- **Useful Variations:** like ceramic, but organic, banded, and ridged.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.2-0.75; ridge normal.
