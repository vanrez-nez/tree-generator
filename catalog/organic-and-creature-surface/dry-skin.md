# Dry Skin

- **Visual Description:** desaturated flesh, tan, gray, or brown surface with wrinkles, pores, flaky dry patches, and red or dark crease tint.
- **Procedural Identity:** wrinkle networks, pore noise, dry flake masks, color mottling.
- **Useful Variations:** like leather, but softer, more porous, and less tanned.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.55-0.95; medium pore/wrinkle normal.
