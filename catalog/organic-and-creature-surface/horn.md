# Horn

- **Visual Description:** translucent-looking brown, cream, black, or amber material with layered bands running lengthwise. Baked version should suggest depth through color banding only.
- **Procedural Identity:** directional curved bands, amber/brown gradients, low pore noise.
- **Useful Variations:** like bone, but glossier, darker-banded, and less porous.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.25-0.65; low normal.
