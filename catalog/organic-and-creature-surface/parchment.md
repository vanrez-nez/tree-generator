# Parchment

- **Visual Description:** aged tan sheet material, #c8a86d to #ead6a8, with fibers, stains, wrinkles, and darker burned or torn-looking edges when used as a surface.
- **Procedural Identity:** fibrous noise, stain masks, wrinkle lines, edge darkening.
- **Useful Variations:** like paper, but warmer, older, and more leathery.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.85-1.0; low fiber normal.
