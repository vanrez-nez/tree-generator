# Wax

- **Visual Description:** cream, yellow, red, or colored soft solid with smooth dents, cloudy translucency implied by color, and slightly greasy roughness variation.
- **Procedural Identity:** smooth broad dents, cloudy internal color patches, scratch/gouge marks.
- **Useful Variations:** like plastic, but softer, warmer, and more dented.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.25-0.65; low dent normal.
