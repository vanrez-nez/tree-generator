# Chitin

- **Visual Description:** insect-like hard shell, often dark brown, black, red, or green, with segmented plates, tiny pits, and glossy worn ridges.
- **Procedural Identity:** plate segmentation, dark cavity seams, glossy high points, fine pitting.
- **Useful Variations:** like painted plastic, but organic, segmented, and subtly porous.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.18-0.65; medium plate normal.
