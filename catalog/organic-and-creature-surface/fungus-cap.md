# Fungus Cap

- **Visual Description:** matte organic cap surface in beige, red, orange, brown, or gray, with speckles, radial streaks, and soft darker bruised patches.
- **Procedural Identity:** radial growth noise, spots, soft color blooms, low rubbery height.
- **Useful Variations:** like suede, but biological, spotted, and radially patterned.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.8-1.0; soft low normal.
