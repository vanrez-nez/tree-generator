# Denim

- **Visual Description:** diagonal twill fabric, usually indigo #1d355b to faded blue #6f87a5, with white thread flecks and worn pale streaks.
- **Procedural Identity:** twill diagonal bands, blue warp/white weft contrast, abrasion masks on ridges.
- **Useful Variations:** like cotton, but diagonal, denser, and blue-white striated.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.85-1.0; fine diagonal normal.
