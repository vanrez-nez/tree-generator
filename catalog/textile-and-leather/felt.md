# Felt

- **Visual Description:** compressed fuzzy fabric with no visible weave, usually solid muted color. Surface is cloudy and dense, with tiny fibers.
- **Procedural Identity:** isotropic fiber noise, no thread grid, subtle nap direction.
- **Useful Variations:** like wool, but flatter, denser, and without woven structure.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 1.0; very fine normal.
