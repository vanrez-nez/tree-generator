# Cracked Leather

- **Visual Description:** brown, black, tan, or oxblood hide with irregular creases, pore dots, worn lighter high points, and dark cracked valleys.
- **Procedural Identity:** organic crack network, pore noise, edge wear masks, broad oiliness roughness variation.
- **Useful Variations:** like vinyl, but warmer, more porous, and irregularly cracked.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.35-0.85; medium crease normal and AO.
