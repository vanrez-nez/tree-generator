# Burlap

- **Visual Description:** open coarse tan weave, #9f8051 to #c0a36c, with thick rope-like threads and dark holes between strands.
- **Procedural Identity:** large grid weave, high thread relief, irregular gaps, straw-colored fibers.
- **Useful Variations:** like coarse linen, but larger, rougher, and visibly porous.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.95-1.0; strong weave normal and AO.
