# Coarse Linen

- **Visual Description:** beige or desaturated dyed fabric with irregular thick threads, visible slubs, and uneven spacing. It looks dry and natural.
- **Procedural Identity:** cross-weave with thread-width jitter, random darker fiber knots, tan gray color scatter.
- **Useful Variations:** like cotton, but rougher, looser, and more uneven.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.95-1.0; medium thread normal.
