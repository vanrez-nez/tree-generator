# Suede

- **Visual Description:** matte soft leather with powdery nap, muted color, and cloudy directional rub marks. It lacks glossy creases.
- **Procedural Identity:** fine fiber noise, directional smudge masks, low pore contrast.
- **Useful Variations:** like felt, but denser, warmer, and leather-colored.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.95-1.0; fine soft normal.
