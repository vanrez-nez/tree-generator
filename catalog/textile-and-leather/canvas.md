# Canvas

- **Visual Description:** heavy plain-weave fabric with thicker yarns than cotton, often khaki, olive, gray, or off-white. It has utilitarian stiffness.
- **Procedural Identity:** regular square weave, medium thread relief, subtle dirt in thread intersections.
- **Useful Variations:** like cotton, but thicker, flatter, and more rugged.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.9-1.0; medium weave normal.
