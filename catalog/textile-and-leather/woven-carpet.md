# Woven Carpet

- **Visual Description:** dense textile pile with short upright fibers, mottled color, and recessed grooves where yarn groups meet.
- **Procedural Identity:** tuft clusters, pile direction patches, color flecks, dark groove AO.
- **Useful Variations:** like wool, but thicker, more spatially clumped, and floor-like.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 1.0; medium soft normal.
