# Velvet

- **Visual Description:** deep soft fabric with darker low-angle-looking zones baked as directional nap bands, commonly burgundy, emerald, black, or royal blue.
- **Procedural Identity:** smooth nap direction masks, soft tonal waves, very low thread visibility.
- **Useful Variations:** like felt, but richer, darker in directional patches, and more luxurious.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; high roughness but strong color/roughness anisotropy baked into texture; soft normal.
