# Silk

- **Visual Description:** smooth fine fabric with subtle thread lines and pearly color shifts baked as broad soft bands. Colors are clean and saturated.
- **Procedural Identity:** ultra-fine weave, broad highlight-like bands in roughness/color, low fuzz.
- **Useful Variations:** like cotton, but smoother, shinier, and less fuzzy.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.25-0.65; very low normal.
