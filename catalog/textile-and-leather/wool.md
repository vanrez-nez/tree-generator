# Wool

- **Visual Description:** fuzzy matte textile with soft heathered color and shallow tangled fibers. Edges between threads are blurred rather than crisp.
- **Procedural Identity:** fiber-noise clumps, low-frequency color heather, soft high-frequency normal.
- **Useful Variations:** like felt, but with more visible fiber direction and woven hints.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 1.0; soft normal; low AO.
