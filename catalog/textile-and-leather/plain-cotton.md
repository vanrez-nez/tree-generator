# Plain Cotton

- **Visual Description:** soft matte fabric with fine perpendicular threads and mild color variation. Common colors include off-white #e8e1d2, navy #26344f, gray #777b7c, and muted red #8d3f37.
- **Procedural Identity:** balanced over-under weave, tiny fiber fuzz, low height contrast.
- **Useful Variations:** like linen, but smoother, softer, and less slubbed.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.9-1.0; fine weave normal.
