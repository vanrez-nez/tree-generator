# Braided Rope

- **Visual Description:** tan, gray, or dyed twisted fiber cords crossing in repeated diagonal bands. Fibers are dry and frayed at small scale.
- **Procedural Identity:** helical or braided diagonal pattern, strand ridges, small fiber hairs, darker valleys.
- **Useful Variations:** like burlap, but organized into large diagonal cord bundles.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.95-1.0; strong directional normal.
