# Exposed Aggregate

- **Visual Description:** concrete matrix with visible rounded stones embedded throughout, often gray cement with tan, white, black, and brown pebble inclusions.
- **Procedural Identity:** pebble cell masks in cement, per-stone color variation, raised/flush aggregate roughness changes.
- **Useful Variations:** like gravel, but locked inside concrete rather than loose.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.55-0.9; medium aggregate normal.
