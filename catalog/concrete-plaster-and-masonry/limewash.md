# Limewash

- **Visual Description:** matte mineral paint over plaster or masonry, with cloudy white, beige, or muted color variations and visible brushy mineral blooms.
- **Procedural Identity:** soft cloudy wash masks, streaked application direction, subtle underlying surface texture.
- **Useful Variations:** like painted plaster, but more mineral, translucent-looking, and uneven.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.9-1.0; low normal.
