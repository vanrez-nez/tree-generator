# Poured Concrete

- **Visual Description:** neutral gray surface, #8a8a82 to #c0bfb6, with cloudy trowel marks, tiny pores, and occasional aggregate specks.
- **Procedural Identity:** large soft gray noise, small pinholes, faint formwork or trowel streaks.
- **Useful Variations:** like limestone, but more uniform, colder, and manufactured.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.65-0.95; low pore normal.
