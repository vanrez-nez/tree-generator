# Plaster

- **Visual Description:** chalky wall surface, off-white, beige, or pale gray, with soft trowel waves and small scratches.
- **Procedural Identity:** broad smeared strokes, fine powder noise, hairline cracks.
- **Useful Variations:** like concrete, but lighter, smoother, and more powdery.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.85-1.0; low normal.
