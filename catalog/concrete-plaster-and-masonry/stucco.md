# Stucco

- **Visual Description:** exterior plaster with raised sandy bumps and irregular swirl or knockdown pattern. It is rougher than interior plaster.
- **Procedural Identity:** granular raised blobs, broad trowel arcs, dark pockets between bumps.
- **Useful Variations:** like plaster, but coarser, more exterior, and more textured.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.9-1.0; strong bump normal.
