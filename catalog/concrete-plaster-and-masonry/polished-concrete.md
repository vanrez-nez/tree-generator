# Polished Concrete

- **Visual Description:** smooth gray concrete with visible aggregate chips under a honed surface. It has muted reflections and low relief.
- **Procedural Identity:** embedded aggregate shapes, broad polish roughness variation, minimal pore bump.
- **Useful Variations:** like terrazzo, but less colorful and more cement-gray.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.22-0.55; low normal.
