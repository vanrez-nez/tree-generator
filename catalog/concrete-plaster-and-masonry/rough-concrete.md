# Rough Concrete

- **Visual Description:** coarse gray cement with exposed sand/aggregate, chipped pits, and darker dirty recesses.
- **Procedural Identity:** aggregate inclusion masks, rough pitting, broad chipped patches.
- **Useful Variations:** like poured concrete, but grainier, more broken, and higher relief.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.85-1.0; medium to strong normal.
