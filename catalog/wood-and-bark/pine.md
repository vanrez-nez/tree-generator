# Pine

- **Visual Description:** pale yellow wood, #d6b66b to #f0d89b, with broad soft growth rings and occasional amber knots. Grain is straighter and lighter than oak.
- **Procedural Identity:** wide ring bands, resin streaks, knot ovals, low pore detail.
- **Useful Variations:** like oak, but lighter, softer, and less porous.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.45-0.8; mild grain normal.
