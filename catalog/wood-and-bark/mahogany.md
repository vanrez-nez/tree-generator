# Mahogany

- **Visual Description:** rich red-brown wood, #6f2d1d to #b35c37, with interlocked ribbon grain and satin depth.
- **Procedural Identity:** red base ramp, alternating ribbon stripes, fine pore lines.
- **Useful Variations:** like walnut, but redder, warmer, and more ribboned.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.3-0.7.
