# Rotten Wood

- **Visual Description:** gray-brown decayed wood with soft crumbly fibers, dark cavities, pale fungal stains, and broken grain continuity.
- **Procedural Identity:** disrupted grain, cavity holes, desaturated color, fungal spot masks.
- **Useful Variations:** like driftwood, but darker, softer, and more decomposed.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.85-1.0; strong cavity normal/AO.
