# Oak

- **Visual Description:** warm tan to medium brown wood, #b17a42 to #6b3f20, with strong open grain, long pores, and ray flecks that cross the grain.
- **Procedural Identity:** directional grain bands, dark open pores, occasional pale medullary ray streaks.
- **Useful Variations:** like pine, but darker, denser, and more porous.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.45-0.85; directional grain normal.
