# Rough Bark

- **Visual Description:** brown-gray outer tree bark with vertical ridges, deep dark fissures, and broken plates. Color ranges from #2b2118 to #8a5a35.
- **Procedural Identity:** vertical ridge fields, deep fissure AO, layered plate breakup, moss or lichen optional as variants.
- **Useful Variations:** like charred wood, but brown, fibrous, and not carbon-black.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.85-1.0; strong normal/height.
