# Cork

- **Visual Description:** tan porous material, #b48755 to #d6b982, with irregular dark pores and compressed speckles. It looks lightweight and spongy.
- **Procedural Identity:** dense random pore cells, tan/yellow base variation, low rounded relief.
- **Useful Variations:** like bark, but flatter, softer, and more uniformly porous.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.9-1.0; medium pore normal.
