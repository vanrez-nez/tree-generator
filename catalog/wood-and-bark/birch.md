# Birch

- **Visual Description:** pale cream wood, #e5d8b5 to #f4ebd0, with fine straight grain and small dark dash marks. It is clean and lightly colored.
- **Procedural Identity:** low-contrast grain, small lenticel-like dashes, subtle yellow-gray bands.
- **Useful Variations:** like maple, but slightly more yellow and dotted with tiny marks.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.45-0.8; low normal.
