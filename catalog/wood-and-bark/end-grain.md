# End Grain

- **Visual Description:** circular or oval growth rings viewed across the cut, with radial cracks and dense pore dots. Rings radiate from one or more centers.
- **Procedural Identity:** concentric ring fields, radial crack masks, pore dots, center color variation.
- **Useful Variations:** like normal wood, but grain runs in circular cross-section instead of lengthwise.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.55-0.9; ring and crack normal.
