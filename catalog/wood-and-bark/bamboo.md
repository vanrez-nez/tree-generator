# Bamboo

- **Visual Description:** pale yellow-green or tan segmented material with long straight fibers and periodic node bands. It reads hollow and fibrous.
- **Procedural Identity:** parallel fiber streaks, horizontal node rings, slight color bands between segments.
- **Useful Variations:** like light wood, but smoother, straighter, and divided by regular nodes.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.35-0.75; fine vertical normal.
