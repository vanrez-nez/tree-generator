# Peeling Bark

- **Visual Description:** bark surface with curled lighter flakes separating from a darker underlayer. Peel edges create thin bright rims and shadowed gaps.
- **Procedural Identity:** elongated peel masks, lifted edge height, inner/outer bark color split.
- **Useful Variations:** like rough bark, but with papery overlapping plates and exposed underbark.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.75-1.0; strong edge AO.
