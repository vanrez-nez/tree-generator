# Plywood

- **Visual Description:** engineered wood with alternating thin layers visible as parallel bands, usually beige to tan. Face may show rotary-cut grain swirls.
- **Procedural Identity:** stacked layer stripes on edge-style surfaces, broad veneer grain on face-style surfaces, glue-line dark bands.
- **Useful Variations:** like pine, but more manufactured and layered.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.45-0.85.
