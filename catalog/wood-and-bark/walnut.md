# Walnut

- **Visual Description:** dark chocolate-brown wood, #3b2316 to #7a4a2b, with flowing darker streaks and occasional purple-gray undertones.
- **Procedural Identity:** wavy grain ribbons, low contrast pores, dark heartwood bands.
- **Useful Variations:** like mahogany, but cooler, darker, and less red.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.35-0.75.
