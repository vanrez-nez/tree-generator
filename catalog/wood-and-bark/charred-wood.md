# Charred Wood

- **Visual Description:** blackened wood, #060504 to #2a2118, with cracked alligator-like carbon plates and warm brown exposed grooves.
- **Procedural Identity:** dark crack cell network, raised carbon islands, subtle underlying grain direction.
- **Useful Variations:** like bark, but burnt, flatter, and split into brittle black plates.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.75-1.0; strong crack normal/AO.
