# Wet Wood

- **Visual Description:** dark saturated wood with deeper brown grain and glossy streaks in low areas. It keeps wood structure but appears heavier and darker.
- **Procedural Identity:** darkened grain mask, roughness lowered in streaks and recesses, subtle water-stain blotches without actual liquid animation.
- **Useful Variations:** like dry wood, but darker, smoother, and more contrasty in grain.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.25-0.7; normal unchanged from base wood.
