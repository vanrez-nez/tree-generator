# Hand-Painted Stone

- **Visual Description:** stone with painterly color bands, exaggerated dark cracks, and light edge strokes baked into base color. It should read illustrated, not scanned.
- **Procedural Identity:** broad brush-like masks, stylized crack outlines, simplified AO in color.
- **Useful Variations:** like granite or slate, but with manually emphasized edges and reduced micro-noise.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.75-1.0; medium simplified normal.
