# Emissive Rune Surface

- **Visual Description:** stone, metal, or ceramic base engraved with glowing symbols or lines. The glow is static and stored in emission, not light-dependent animation.
- **Procedural Identity:** base material substrate, engraved line mask, bright emission color, dark groove AO.
- **Useful Variations:** like crystal vein stone, but with intentional glyph geometry rather than natural mineral seams.
- **Typical Channels:** base channels from substrate; emission for glyphs; groove normal/AO.
