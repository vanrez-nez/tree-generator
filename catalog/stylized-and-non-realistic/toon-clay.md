# Toon Clay

- **Visual Description:** simplified matte clay with broad color zones, soft rounded dents, and intentionally low detail. Colors are clean and readable.
- **Procedural Identity:** low-frequency blotches, smooth dents, reduced grain, high roughness.
- **Useful Variations:** like real clay, but cleaner, flatter, and more graphic.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.9-1.0; low soft normal.
