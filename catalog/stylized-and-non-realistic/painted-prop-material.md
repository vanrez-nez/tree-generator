# Painted Prop Material

- **Visual Description:** intentionally simplified game-prop surface with hand-painted highlights, edge wear, and AO baked into color. It can emulate wood, metal, or stone while staying stylized.
- **Procedural Identity:** curvature-like edge highlights, hand-painted dirt gradients, simplified color ramps.
- **Useful Variations:** like real painted wood or metal, but cleaner, chunkier, and designed for readability at distance.
- **Typical Channels:** material-dependent metallic; roughness mostly high; normal simplified.
