# Arcane Metal

- **Visual Description:** dark or bright metal with unnatural colored oxidation, engraved geometric lines, and static emissive accents. It should still obey a metallic base.
- **Procedural Identity:** metallic substrate, geometric masks, colored tarnish, emission stripes or symbols.
- **Useful Variations:** like gunmetal or brass, but with fantasy color accents and precise engraved linework.
- **Typical Channels:** metallic 1.0 except paint/oxide; roughness 0.2-0.75; optional emission.
