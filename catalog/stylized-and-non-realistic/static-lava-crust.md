# Static Lava Crust

- **Visual Description:** black cracked volcanic crust with orange or red glowing fissures, represented as a frozen baked surface with no flow.
- **Procedural Identity:** dark basalt plates, branching hot cracks, emission only in fissures, heat-dark color halo.
- **Useful Variations:** like cracked clay, but black, glassier, and emissive in cracks.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic crust; roughness 0.55-0.95; strong crack normal/AO; emission in fissures.
