# Magical Crystal

- **Visual Description:** saturated faceted mineral in cyan, violet, emerald, amber, or red, with bright internal cracks and optional static emission lines.
- **Procedural Identity:** angular facet cells, inner vein lines, bright core gradients, emissive crack mask.
- **Useful Variations:** like quartz, but more saturated, cleaner, and optionally glowing.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.08-0.35; faceted normal; optional emission.
