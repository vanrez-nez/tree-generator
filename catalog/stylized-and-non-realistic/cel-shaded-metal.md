# Cel-Shaded Metal

- **Visual Description:** metal surface with flat tonal bands and graphic scratches instead of physically smooth gradients. Color can be blue-gray steel, gold, or dark gunmetal.
- **Procedural Identity:** stepped value ramps, clean scratch strokes, simplified roughness blocks.
- **Useful Variations:** like brushed steel, but posterized and less physically continuous.
- **Typical Channels:** metallic 1.0; roughness in broad bands; low normal.
