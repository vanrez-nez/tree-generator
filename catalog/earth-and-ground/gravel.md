# Gravel

- **Visual Description:** dense field of small stones, typically gray, tan, brown, and off-white, each with distinct edges and slight shadowing. Stone sizes vary from 3 mm to 40 mm visually.
- **Procedural Identity:** packed Voronoi pebble shapes, per-stone color jitter, raised pebble centers, dark contact AO.
- **Useful Variations:** like rocky terrain, but made of separated loose fragments instead of continuous bedrock.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.65-0.95; strong normal/height; strong contact AO.
