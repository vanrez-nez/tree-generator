# Sand

- **Visual Description:** pale beige to golden grains, #c8b37a to #e5d5a4, with tiny granular speckles and optional wind ripples. It should not contain large organic clumps.
- **Procedural Identity:** fine grain noise, shallow sinusoidal ripple bands, slightly darker troughs.
- **Useful Variations:** like dry soil, but lighter, finer, and more uniform; like sandstone, but loose and without cemented layers.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.9-1.0; very fine normal.
