# Packed Snow

- **Visual Description:** off-white compressed surface, #d8dde0 to #f6f7f5, with blue-gray depressions and dirty beige flecks. It is matte except for slightly polished compressed ridges.
- **Procedural Identity:** soft cellular compression, blue shadow tint in cavities, granular icy specks.
- **Useful Variations:** like chalk, but colder in shadows and more powder-granular.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.55-0.95; low to medium normal; AO cool and subtle.
