# Leaf Litter

- **Visual Description:** overlapping flattened leaf fragments in brown, ochre, tan, and dark red, with curled edges and vein marks. It reads as a noisy organic ground layer, not soil.
- **Procedural Identity:** random elongated leaf silhouettes, visible midrib/side veins, edge darkening, partial decay spots.
- **Useful Variations:** like bark chips, but flatter, thinner, and more leaf-shaped.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.75-0.95; medium height layering; AO under overlaps.
