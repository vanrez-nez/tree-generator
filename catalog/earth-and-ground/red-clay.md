# Red Clay

- **Visual Description:** saturated terracotta earth, #8f3f25 to #bf5b32, with soft smears and shallow compression marks. It reads warmer and denser than generic soil.
- **Procedural Identity:** red-orange base ramp, smooth broad clumps, occasional pale dry streaks, minimal pebble contrast.
- **Useful Variations:** like cracked clay, but less fractured and more plastic; like terracotta, but un-fired and less uniform.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.7-0.95; low to medium normal.
