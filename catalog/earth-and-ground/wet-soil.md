# Wet Soil

- **Visual Description:** dark brown to near-black earth, #1f1710 to #4a2e1d, with glossy patches between clumps. The surface appears heavier and more cohesive than dry soil.
- **Procedural Identity:** darker cavity masks, larger rounded clumps, reduced powder noise, roughness variation where wet patches sit in depressions.
- **Useful Variations:** like dry soil, but darker, smoother in low areas, and more reflective in puddle-like stains without requiring actual water.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.35-0.75; moderate normal; AO strong in clump seams.
