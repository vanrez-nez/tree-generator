# Compact Dirt Path

- **Visual Description:** pressed brown-gray ground, #514336 to #7b6652, with flattened smooth zones, shallow wheel or foot scuffs, and embedded small stones.
- **Procedural Identity:** directional smear noise, flattened center bands, scattered pebble masks, faint parallel rut grooves.
- **Useful Variations:** like dry soil, but smoother, lower relief, and more horizontally scuffed.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.75-0.95; weak normal except pebbles and ruts.
