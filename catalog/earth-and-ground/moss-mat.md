# Moss Mat

- **Visual Description:** dense green cushion, #2f4d1f to #7a8f35, with tiny tufted clusters and dark damp gaps. It appears soft, fuzzy, and uneven.
- **Procedural Identity:** clustered blob noise, fine hair-like height breakup, yellow-green tips over dark underlayer.
- **Useful Variations:** like grass, but shorter, denser, and less individually bladed.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; roughness 0.9-1.0; soft high-frequency normal; strong cavity AO.
