# Volcanic Ash

- **Visual Description:** charcoal-gray powder, #1c1c1c to #55514b, with soft dusty gradients and occasional black glassy grains. It reads dry and lightweight.
- **Procedural Identity:** low-contrast smoky noise, black speck inclusion, subtle wind-streak direction.
- **Useful Variations:** like dry soil, but cooler, darker, and finer; like basalt powder, but less stony.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; high roughness; low normal.
