# Dry Soil

- **Visual Description:** matte dusty brown surface, usually #6b4a2e to #9a744a, with fine powder speckling and scattered darker compacted grains. Height variation is shallow and granular, not cracked.
- **Procedural Identity:** low-frequency color blotches mixed with high-frequency grain noise; sparse pebble flecks under 5% coverage.
- **Useful Variations:** like compact dirt, but lighter, dustier, and less compressed; like sand, but darker and more clumped.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; high roughness 0.85-1.0; weak normal; soft AO in small grain pockets.
