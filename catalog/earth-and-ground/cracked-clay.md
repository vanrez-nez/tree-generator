# Cracked Clay

- **Visual Description:** flat tan, ochre, or red-brown plates separated by dark polygonal cracks. Plates are broad and slightly curled at edges, with fine powder on top.
- **Procedural Identity:** Voronoi plate cells, thin dark crack lines, raised or curled plate centers, edge AO.
- **Useful Variations:** like dried mud, but cleaner, flatter, and divided into harder polygon plates.
- **Typical Channels:** non-metallic; high roughness; medium height with deep narrow crack AO.
